Cafes In Pondicherry - The Heart Of French Cafes

La Maison Rose is a boutique styled restaurant which depicts French architecture in its grandeur. The backdrop of this cafe in Pondicherry is a historic villa which lets you enjoy its French colonial style and offers a special feeling of your soul being at bliss. If you prefer dining amidst nature's serene beauty, then this place would complete awe you. The tables are arranged neatly under mango trees which offer a great ambience and perfect solace from the outside world.


Le cafe Sited on the promenade that lines the shore of the French Quarter, this squat white building – an unmissable landmark – offers some of the best sea-facing seats you’re going to get. The food isn’t anything to write home about, but this is an allowance we’re willing to make to soak in the calming sound of waves crashing against the rocky shore. Get here early for sunrise or sunset (the café is open 24x7); it’s often full up. Order a cup of coffee, fire up your Instagram, and get ready to capture those gorgeous, #nofilterneeded views. Address: No 40, Goubert Avenue, Near Gandhi Statue, French Quarter, Pondicherry 605002

Cafe de Flore It is a small café at Pondy's Alliance Francaise. Simple yet delicious food with a lovely courtyard is what this café offers proudly. Mouthwatering pasta, fresh burgers made using fresh farm veggies and delicious Nutella crepes would surely top your list.

Auroville bakery in the serene Auroville township of Pondicherry, it is a small bakery cum cafe in Auroville with the choicest items on the menu. Although the options are limited you can definitely expect to have a hearty and healthy breakfast. It is a popular hotspot for croissants and chocolate offerings in the city.
